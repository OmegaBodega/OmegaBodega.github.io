1.00 Forgot Her Name
  Man always calls his wife by such names as darling,
sweetheart, cher. Neighbor says “That is so sweet”.
Man says “Thank you but the truth of the matter is I
forgot her name”.
   
2.00 Trade Wife For Dog
  Boudreaux says "Thibodeaux, that is a beautiful new
dog you have there". Thibodeaux says " Thank you, I
got that for my wife". Boudreaux says " Mais you
made a good trade yea".
   
3.00 Parking Space, Found By You Or God? Thanks to Craig Huval
  Boudreau going to Saints football game. Looking for
parking spot. Finally prays to God; please help me
find a parking spot. Just about that time somebody
pulls out and Boudreau pulls in. Boudreau says;
that's all right God I found a spot.
   
4.00 Sorry I Love You - Good Joke For Toast
  There is two parts to the recipe for happiness. First
part is three words; I love you. Second part is two
words I’m sorry. I know it works because every
morning my wife says "I’m sorry I love you".
   
5.00 You Or Wine Talking
  Husband and wife are having romantic dinner with
wine. Wife says "I love you. I don't know what I
would do without you". Husband says "Is that you
talking or the wine?" Wife says "It is me talking to
the wine."
